Introduction

Like all game syncs, a trigger is a Wwise element that is first called by the game, then defines a specific response in Wwise to accommodate the game action that required it to be called. More specifically, in interactive music a trigger responds to a spontaneous occurrence in the game and launches a stinger. The stinger (a brief musical phrase) is mixed with the currently-playing music, as a musical reaction to an in-game event. For example, when a ninja draws their weapon, you might want to insert a musical sforzando-type progression over the music to add even more impact to the scene. The game would call the trigger which in turn would launch the stinger and your music clip would play over the ongoing score.
