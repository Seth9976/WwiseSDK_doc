Modeling Sound Propagation from Other Rooms

With Wwise Acoustics Rooms and Portals, the Rooms and Portals abstraction manages sound propagation in all Rooms except for the Room that contains the listener. An emitter in another Room reaches the listener through Portals, and their associated diffraction, by transmission through "walls." Ensure that Enable Diffraction and Transmission is selected in the Positioning tab of each sound that needs to be propagated.
