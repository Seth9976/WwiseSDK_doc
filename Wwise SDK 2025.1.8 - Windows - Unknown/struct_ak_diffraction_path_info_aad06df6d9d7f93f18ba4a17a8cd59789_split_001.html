◆ totLength

AkReal32 AkDiffractionPathInfo::totLength

Total path length Represents the sum of the length of the individual segments between nodes, with a correction factor applied for diffraction. The correction factor simulates the phenomenon where by diffracted sound waves decay faster than incident sound waves and can be customized in the spatial audio init settings.
