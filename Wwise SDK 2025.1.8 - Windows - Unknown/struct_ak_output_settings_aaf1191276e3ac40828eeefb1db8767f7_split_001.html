enum AkPanningRule AkOutputSettings::ePanningRule

Rule for 3D panning of signals routed to a stereo bus. In AkPanningRule_Speakers mode, the angle of the front loudspeakers (uSpeakerAngles[0]) is used. In AkPanningRule_Headphones mode, the speaker angles are superseded by constant power panning between two virtual microphones spaced 180 degrees apart.
