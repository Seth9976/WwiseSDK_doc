struct AkVector64 AkDiffractionPathInfo::nodes[AK_MAX_SOUND_PROPAGATION_DEPTH]

Diffraction points along the path. nodes[0] is the point closest to the listener; nodes[numNodes-1] is the point closest to the emitter. Neither the emitter position nor the listener position are represented in this array.
