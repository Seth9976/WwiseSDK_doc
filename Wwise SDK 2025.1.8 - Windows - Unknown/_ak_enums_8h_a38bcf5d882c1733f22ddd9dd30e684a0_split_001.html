enum AkEngineRenderingMode

Rendering modes of the overall Sound Engine.

Enumerator
AkEngineRenderingMode_Online 

Regular operation, based on real audio sink cadence.

AkEngineRenderingMode_Offline 

No audio output, rendering as fast as possible, every operation is serialized (streaming, monitoring, bank loads, etc)

AkEngineRenderingMode_OfflineThreaded 

No audio output, rendering as fast as possible, but regular threaded operations still happen in threads.
