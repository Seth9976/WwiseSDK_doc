Combining the Geometric APIs with Rooms and Portals

Rooms and Portals in Wwise Acoustics work in conjunction with the geometric APIs for reflection and diffraction. The Rooms and Portals network can be thought of a high level abstraction (or as a low level-of-detail version) of the surrounding geometry. With care, the combination of Rooms and Portals with level geometry can result in an acoustic simulation that is both detailed and efficient.
