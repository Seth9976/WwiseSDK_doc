Direct Path Diffraction Interaction with Wwise Acoustics Rooms and Portals

With Rooms and Portals (Rooms and Portals), Portals also model diffraction of direct sounds in adjacent Rooms. The two systems complement each other in that no geometry-driven diffraction paths are searched for emitters that are not in the same Room as the listener. Provided that Rooms and Portals are much more efficient to calculate than geometry, it is beneficial to use both systems together to reduce computational complexity.
