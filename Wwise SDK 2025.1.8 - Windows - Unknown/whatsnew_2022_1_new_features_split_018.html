WAQL

The overall performance of WAQL has been improved, along with the following additions:

  • Added the "duration" accessor to find objects matching the duration specified. For example: $ from type sound where duration.max > 2.
  • Exposed mute and solo states with isExplicitMute, isExplicitSolo, isImplicitSolo, isImplicitMute. For example: $ where isExplicitMute.
