Bug Fixes

  • WG-56196 Fixed: Crash in Advanced Profiler view on Wwise shutdown.
  • WG-57257 Fixed: Crash on Windows when list of audio devices is changed.
  • WG-57385 Fixed: Crash when using the Impacter plug-in with corrupted .wav files.
  • WG-57483 Fixed: Crash when calling a StopAll operation on paused Interactive Music objects after clearing banks.
  • WG-57532 Fixed: Crash while using the Profiler and Voice Inspector.

  • WG-57702 Fixed: Inaccurate gain evaluation in Compressor on Ambisonics configurations.
  • WG-57739 Fixed: Loudness Metering of configurations larger than 5.1 does not use standard BS.1770-4 channel weighting.
  • WG-57851 Fixed: (Spatial Audio) When two portals are located close together and connect more than two rooms, they might interfere with each other and cause unexpected aux send, diffraction and transmission results.
  • WG-58004 Fixed: Crash when Windows output sound device is removed.
