◆ SetOfflineRenderingFrameTime()

AKSOUNDENGINE_API AKRESULT AK::SoundEngine::SetOfflineRenderingFrameTime ( AkReal32  in_fFrameTimeInSeconds)

Sets the offline rendering frame time in seconds. When offline rendering is enabled, every call to RenderAudio() will generate sample data as if this much time has elapsed. If the frame time argument is less than or equal to zero, every call to RenderAudio() will generate one audio buffer.
