AkUInt32 AkSpatialAudioInitSettings::uClusteringMinPoints

[Experimental] Minimum number of emitters in a cluster. Default value is 0. Values less than 2 disable the clustering. Note: Emitters with multi-positions are not clustered and are treated as independant emitters. Note: Changing an emitter from single to multi-positions with load balancing enabled (see AkSpatialAudioInitSettings::uLoadBalancingSpread) might lead to unknown behaviors for a few frames.
