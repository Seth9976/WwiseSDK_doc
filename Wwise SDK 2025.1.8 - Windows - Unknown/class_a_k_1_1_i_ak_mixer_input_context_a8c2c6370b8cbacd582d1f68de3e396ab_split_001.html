virtual Ak3DPositionType AK::IAkMixerInputContext::Get3DPositionType ( )
pure virtual

Get whether the emitter position is defined by the game alone (AK_3DPositionType_Emitter), or if it is further automated (AK_3DPositionType_EmitterWithAutomation, AK_3DPositionType_ListenerWithAutomation). The resulting 3D position(s) may be obtained by Get3DPosition(), and used for 3D spatialization or attenuation.
