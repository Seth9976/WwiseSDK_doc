AkReal32 AkAuxSendValue::fControlValue

A value in the range [0.0f:16.0f] ( -∞ dB to +24 dB). Represents the attenuation or amplification factor applied to the volume of the sound going through the auxiliary bus. A value greater than 1.0f will amplify the sound.
